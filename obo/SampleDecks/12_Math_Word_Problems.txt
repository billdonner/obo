Title: Math Word Problems

Q: Mia has 12 stickers and gives 5 away. How many left? | A: 7
Q: A class has 24 students. 8 are absent. How many are there? | A: 16
Q: 3 boxes have 6 pencils each. How many pencils? | A: 18
Q: A book costs $4. How much for 5 books? | A: $20
Q: A recipe needs 2 cups of milk. How many for 3 batches? | A: 6 cups
Q: A bus has 30 seats. 12 are filled. How many empty? | A: 18
Q: Liam runs 2 miles each day for 4 days. Total? | A: 8 miles
Q: A rope is 15 feet long. Cut off 6 feet. Length left? | A: 9 feet
Q: A jar has 40 marbles. 10 are blue. How many are not blue? | A: 30
Q: A team scored 18 points, then 7 more. Total? | A: 25
Q: There are 5 rows of chairs with 8 chairs each. Total? | A: 40
Q: A farmer has 27 apples and sells 9. How many left? | A: 18
Q: A class reads 10 pages a day for 6 days. Total pages? | A: 60
Q: A toy costs $7. If you have $20, how much left? | A: $13
Q: A movie is 90 minutes. You watched 35. How much left? | A: 55 minutes
Q: A pack has 12 crayons. You have 3 packs. Total? | A: 36
Q: A yard is 36 inches. How many inches in 2 yards? | A: 72
Q: A trip is 120 miles. You drove 45. Miles left? | A: 75
Q: A jar holds 25 cookies. You eat 4. How many left? | A: 21
Q: A runner does 3 laps. Each lap is 400 meters. Total? | A: 1200 meters
