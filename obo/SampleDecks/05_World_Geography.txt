Title: World Geography

Q: What is the largest continent? | A: Asia
Q: What is the smallest continent? | A: Australia
Q: What is the longest river in the world? | A: The Nile
Q: What is the largest ocean? | A: The Pacific Ocean
Q: What is the highest mountain? | A: Mount Everest
Q: Which continent is the Sahara Desert on? | A: Africa
Q: Which country is shaped like a boot? | A: Italy
Q: Which line divides Earth into Northern and Southern Hemispheres? | A: The Equator
Q: Which line divides East and West longitudes? | A: The Prime Meridian
Q: What is a group of islands called? | A: An archipelago
Q: What do we call a large area of flat land? | A: A plain
Q: What do we call a V-shaped valley made by rivers? | A: A canyon
Q: What is a narrow body of water connecting two larger bodies? | A: A strait
Q: What is a large area of ice called? | A: A glacier
Q: Which continent has the most countries? | A: Africa
Q: What is the capital of France? | A: Paris
Q: What is the capital of Japan? | A: Tokyo
Q: What is the capital of Brazil? | A: Brasilia
Q: What is the largest country by area? | A: Russia
Q: Which continent has the Amazon rainforest? | A: South America
