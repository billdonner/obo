Title: Simple Machines

Q: Name a machine that helps lift heavy things with a bar. | A: A lever
Q: What simple machine is a ramp? | A: An inclined plane
Q: What simple machine is a wheel and axle? | A: A wheel and axle
Q: What simple machine can split things apart? | A: A wedge
Q: What simple machine lifts with a rope? | A: A pulley
Q: What simple machine is a screw? | A: A screw
Q: What part of a lever is the pivot point? | A: The fulcrum
Q: What simple machine is used to cut wood? | A: A wedge
Q: What does a pulley change? | A: The direction of force
Q: What does a wheel and axle reduce? | A: Friction
Q: What is a doorknob an example of? | A: A wheel and axle
Q: What is a seesaw an example of? | A: A lever
Q: What is a slide an example of? | A: An inclined plane
Q: What is a screwdriver an example of? | A: A wheel and axle
Q: What simple machine is a knife? | A: A wedge
Q: What simple machine is a jar lid? | A: A screw
Q: What simple machine is a flagpole? | A: A pulley
Q: What simple machine is a crowbar? | A: A lever
Q: What is the benefit of simple machines? | A: They make work easier
Q: What is friction? | A: A force that resists motion
