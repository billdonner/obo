Title: U.S. History Snapshots

Q: Who was the first U.S. President? | A: George Washington
Q: What year did the Declaration of Independence get signed? | A: 1776
Q: What document starts with 'We the People'? | A: The Constitution
Q: What was the Civil War fought between? | A: The Union and the Confederacy
Q: Who was President during the Civil War? | A: Abraham Lincoln
Q: What famous speech starts with 'I have a dream'? | A: Martin Luther King Jr.'s speech
Q: What ocean is on the U.S. west coast? | A: The Pacific Ocean
Q: What ocean is on the U.S. east coast? | A: The Atlantic Ocean
Q: What is the capital of the United States? | A: Washington, D.C.
Q: What is the Statue of Liberty a symbol of? | A: Freedom
Q: What holiday celebrates the Declaration of Independence? | A: The Fourth of July
Q: What was the name of the first U.S. space program? | A: Mercury
Q: Which war was fought from 1939 to 1945? | A: World War II
Q: What is the name of the U.S. flag? | A: The Stars and Stripes
Q: Who wrote the Declaration of Independence? | A: Thomas Jefferson
Q: Which state was the first to join the Union? | A: Delaware
Q: What is the nickname of the U.S. Constitution? | A: The supreme law of the land
Q: What was the Underground Railroad used for? | A: Helping enslaved people escape
Q: Who is called the Father of the Constitution? | A: James Madison
Q: What is the national anthem called? | A: The Star-Spangled Banner
