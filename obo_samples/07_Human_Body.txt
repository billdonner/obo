Title: Human Body

Q: What organ pumps blood? | A: The heart
Q: What organ helps you breathe? | A: The lungs
Q: What is the largest organ? | A: The skin
Q: What organ controls your thoughts? | A: The brain
Q: What do muscles do? | A: They help you move
Q: What do bones do? | A: They support your body
Q: What is the longest bone in your body? | A: The femur
Q: What are red blood cells for? | A: Carrying oxygen
Q: What does the stomach do? | A: Breaks down food
Q: What does the small intestine do? | A: Absorbs nutrients
Q: What helps protect your brain? | A: The skull
Q: What does the spine protect? | A: The spinal cord
Q: What are joints? | A: Places where bones meet
Q: What is your body's defense system called? | A: The immune system
Q: What is the liquid part of blood called? | A: Plasma
Q: What do kidneys do? | A: Filter waste
Q: What is the name of the tube for air to your lungs? | A: The trachea
Q: What are the tiny air sacs in lungs called? | A: Alveoli
Q: What organ stores urine? | A: The bladder
Q: What is the main job of the skeleton? | A: Support and protection
