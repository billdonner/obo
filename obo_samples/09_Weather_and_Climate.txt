Title: Weather & Climate

Q: What is the difference between weather and climate? | A: Weather is daily; climate is long-term
Q: What tool measures temperature? | A: A thermometer
Q: What tool measures wind speed? | A: An anemometer
Q: What tool measures rainfall? | A: A rain gauge
Q: What do we call water vapor in the air? | A: Humidity
Q: What is a cloud made of? | A: Tiny water droplets or ice
Q: What causes thunder? | A: Lightning heating the air
Q: What is a tornado? | A: A spinning funnel of wind
Q: What is a hurricane? | A: A big storm over warm ocean
Q: What is fog? | A: A cloud at ground level
Q: What is sleet? | A: Frozen rain
Q: What is hail? | A: Balls of ice
Q: What is a drought? | A: A long time with little rain
Q: What is a flood? | A: Too much water on land
Q: What is a rainbow? | A: Light spread out by water drops
Q: What is the water cycle step when water turns into vapor? | A: Evaporation
Q: What is the step when vapor turns to droplets? | A: Condensation
Q: What is the step when water falls from clouds? | A: Precipitation
Q: What is the step when water gathers in rivers or oceans? | A: Collection
Q: What does the Sun do in the water cycle? | A: Provides energy
