Title: Art & Color Theory

Q: What are the primary colors? | A: Red, blue, yellow
Q: What do you get when you mix red and blue? | A: Purple
Q: What do you get when you mix blue and yellow? | A: Green
Q: What do you get when you mix red and yellow? | A: Orange
Q: What are secondary colors? | A: Green, orange, purple
Q: What is a warm color? | A: Red, orange, or yellow
Q: What is a cool color? | A: Blue, green, or purple
Q: What is a complementary color? | A: Colors opposite on the color wheel
Q: What is a tint? | A: A color with white added
Q: What is a shade? | A: A color with black added
Q: What is a line? | A: A mark with length
Q: What is a shape? | A: A closed line
Q: What is texture? | A: How something feels or looks
Q: What is pattern? | A: A repeated design
Q: What is perspective? | A: Showing depth
Q: What is symmetry? | A: Balanced parts
Q: What is contrast? | A: Difference between light and dark
Q: What is a collage? | A: Art made by gluing materials
Q: What is a sculpture? | A: 3D art
Q: What is a palette? | A: A surface for mixing paint
